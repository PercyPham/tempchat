2. Description of Service

TempChat is a zero-knowledge, ephemeral group chat service. It allows users to create temporary, encrypted chat rooms accessible via a unique URL and QR code. Key characteristics of the service:

  • No user accounts, email addresses, or personal identifiers are required to use TempChat.
  • All messages and display names are encrypted client-side in the user's browser before transmission. TempChat's servers store and transmit only opaque ciphertext.
  • Rooms are automatically and permanently deleted upon expiry. All associated data (messages, member metadata, encryption artifacts) is purged from our servers and cannot be recovered.
  • The encryption key for a room is embedded in the room URL fragment and is never transmitted to TempChat's servers.

3. No Account Requirement

5. Room Lifecycle and Data Retention

Free rooms are automatically deleted 3 hours after creation. Boosted rooms are deleted at the end of their boosted lifetime (up to 7 days for Pro Boost). Upon deletion:

  • All room data stored on TempChat's servers — including ciphertext messages, member identifiers, and room metadata — is permanently purged.
  • No backups or archives of room content are retained by TempChat after expiry.
  • Locally stored data (the room key, userId, and display name in your browser's localStorage) is your responsibility. Clearing your browser storage or manually leaving a room removes this data from your device.

TempChat does not guarantee any minimum uptime or room availability. Rooms may become inaccessible before their scheduled expiry due to service interruptions, infrastructure failures, or other unforeseen circumstances.
